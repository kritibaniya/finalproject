FAQ - frequently asked questions

Can I use the app without creating a user? / Why do I need to create a user to use this app?

No, you can not use the app without creating a user. u can authorize yourself later. however u can not use it fully or see everyhting wothpough athorise


Why do I need to authorize myself?

to users sefety, slik at de vet at de er ekte. so the users know that person er ekte. If something ubehagelig happens we can contact. Why can I not post or look at someone’s post without authorizing my account?


How will my information be secured? (Klarte ikke å formulere den)

ingen andre kan stjele din identitet.


Do you need a profile picture of me? Will my photo be shown to everybody?

Nei, trenger ikke. blir vist bare de som har autorisert seg


What kind of information about me will be visible to others?

navn må. bildet du kan velge. Adresse område/kommune/by. if u have chosen tge option dine in, the giver will give their adress by their own will when they are contacted regarding their post


Who can I send messages to?

to the previous giver you have contacted and also to the ones you will contact in the future


I changed the view type for “Explore Food” from list to map. How can I change it back to list type?

It is a button on the htop right corner. You can click on the button to go back to list menu. This is also the button you can click on to get the map menu.


What will happen to my post after the time limit expires?

It will not be visible to the other users. And you will get the option to renew it if the food still is edible, or else you can delete it.


The food I want to post is neither breakfast, lunch, dinner or groceries. What should I choose?

If the food is already prepared or cooked, you can post it under meals. If it is raw you can post it under groceries.


Do I have to eat the food with the host if he/she has posted in dine in?

You can contact the host and ask them about it.


Can I trust the users who have posted are real?

Yes, that is why we have authorizing. I tilfelle du oppelever noe dårlig så er det bare å kontakte.


How can I know if the food is edible?

the giver is responsible for food being quality assured. If not we will take actions, and they will face the consquences. the takers have their responsibilty to ensure that the food is edible before consuming it. Watch it, smell it and taste it.


What if I get food poisoning?

Hvis du er syk og mistenker at det er forårsaket av mat eller drikke, kontakt lege. Please contact us immidietally, so we can assist you and do our own inquiery.


I had a bad experience with a user. What can I do? What should I do? Who should I tell?

d finnes en funksjon experience in the menu, hvor du kan dele dine opplevelser. we will review it and follow the case further.


Why don't you guys have a reward system like many other apps?

bcuz our mission is to reduce food svin, og om vi skulle ha rewarding system hvor folk skulle få poeng når de legger ut mat. En konsekvens av dette kunne være at folk bevisst bevisst lager ekstra porsjon med mat, bare for å poste det u i appen.OG dette vil gå imot formålet vårt.

About


We are The Neat, and our job is to do our best to keep the earth tidy and neat. The Neat is a well-developed app where you can share your food with people near you. Hereby, we want to accomplish less waste of food. We believe our small step will contribute a lot in this huge issue of food waste. Don’t waste your food and time, join us!

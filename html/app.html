Get started


When you first open the app you will be brought to the sign in page, where you can sign in with your account. If you dont have an accoount, you can click the "Sign up" button on the bottom. After filling out the sign up form you will be taken to a site which will tell you to authorize yourself. You can read more about why this is needed here. If you are not able to authorize yourself at this time, you can skip and do it later, but you will not be able to use the app to the fullest until you have finifhes your account.

yo idk yet gimme a sec


After signing in you will be taken to he homepage, here you can see a list of different items that have been uploaded by other users near you. As well as you can see all the items listed, your can also choose to see a map over the different spots a “Giver” has posted food, as well as the food trucks in the area.

upload and choose


There is a big black button on the app, with a plus sign on it which tells the users that it is here they can upload their food. When they click that button, they will be taken to the “Upload” site. Here you must insert the name of the dish, the location, a description, what kind of food you are giving away, as well choose if you wish to have the “Taker” dine in or not. There is also possible to upload a picture of your dish, but it is not mandatory. You will see a very similar layout when you choose a dish from the list. Here you can see all the information about the food you have chosen, and at the bottom you can click on the “Contact Giver” button, if this is something you wish to eat.

Our Next Step


When we will be able to continue with our next step, the layout of the app will still be the same, with only a few changes. It will then have another option under "Upload" called "Foodlocker", and the nearest lockers will be placed on your map.

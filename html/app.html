Get Started


The Neat contains alot of different features, but to be able to have access to these features you need to be signed it. This is why the first page your will see when opening up the app for the first time is the "Sign in" page, where you can sign in by using your "username" and "password". But if you are new to the app, and you have not signed up earlier, you can click the "Sign up" which is placed on the bottom of the screen. It will take you only a few minutes to sign up and get started!

Authentication


After you have finished signing up, you will be taken to another site, where you will be asked to authenticate yourself. You can read more about why this is needed [here]. But it is not always easy to authenticate yourself at any time, which is why you can skip this part for now and continue to the app. Without authentication you can not use the app to the fullest, but you can see the functions of the app. But to be able to upload or to choose any of the dishes that have been uploaded, have too authenticate yourself.

Main Page


After signing in you will be taken to the main page. Here you will see a list of different items that the users around you have posted. Here you can see the food listed up, but there is also an option to see the food placed on a app. The app makes it easier to see which directions the dishes are. On this map you can also see where the foodtrucks are located.

Upload & Interested


On the main page there is a black button at the bootom, with a plus sign on it, which takes you directly to a page where you can upload a dish or any kind of grocery item. When uploading you can to state the name of the item, as well as the location the person can pick it up from. There is also a input-box for "description", where you have to give a description of your item. In addition to that, you must choose if you wish to have the “Taker” dine in or not. There is also an option where you can upload a picture of your dish, but this is not mandatory. You will find that the layout of the site that shows when you choose a dish is very similar to the upload site. Here you will se the same information as the upload site, but placed in a more informative way. If you are interessed in this item, you can click the “Contact Giver” button, where you can have a chat with the giver. Here you can further talk with the giver about time and place.

Our Next Step


When we will be able to continue with our next step, the layout of the app will still be the same, with only a few changes. It will then have another option under "Upload" called "Foodlocker", and the nearest lockers will be placed on your map.

About


We are The Neat, and our job is to do our best to keep the earth tidy and neat. The Neat is a well-developed app where you can share your food with people near you. Hereby, we want to accomplish less waste of food. We believe our small step will contribute a lot in this huge issue of food waste. Don’t waste your food and time, join us!

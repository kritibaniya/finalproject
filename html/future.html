The future is bright

Our Next Solution


The next step we wish to take with our project is aimed at smaller communities. Considering that we have food trucks in central parts of the country, we wanted to find a solution for smaller areas and more rural areas.


Those who live in the more sentral parts, and do not wish to enter their adress, or have anyone at their house can use the foodtrucks that are placed around. But this is not possible for those who live in smaller areas. This is why our next step is to create "Foodlockers". These lockers will have a built-in cooling element, so that the food does not get bad. The lockers will be located in central locations in the various local communities. For the users to have access to the food in the lockers, they will get a passcode from the "giver" that they can use to unlock the lockers. This assures that no one can take anyone elses food, as well as ruin the food that is left unattended.


The App


When we will be able to continue with our next step, the layout of the app will still be the same, but with a few changes. On the upload page there will be an option for "foodlocker" where the giver can choose that as a location. As well as this, there will be icons on the map which will show you where your nearest foodlocker is.

Solution

Our app makes it possible for everyone to share their foods. But some people might not live in the more populated locations, such as the city, where there are food trucks. As well as they might not want to give their address to unknown people. This is why we want out next step to be "foodlockers", where
Our next step with our project is aimed at smaller communities. Considering that we have food trucks in central parts of the country, we wanted to find a solution for smaller areas.


Our idea is based on compartments with built-in cooling element, so that the food does not get bad. The lockers will be located in central locations in the various local communities. For example, if you as a giver have posted a post on the app, and someone has shown interest in it. Instead of the person visiting your house, you can leave the food in one of the lockers, lock it, and send the code and locker number to the person. This allows them to drop by and pick up the food when it suits them best.


The App


When we will be able to continue with our next step, the layout of the app will still be the same, with only a few changes. It will then have another option under "Upload" called "Foodlocker", and the nearest lockers will be placed on your map.

About


We are The Neat, and our job is to do our best to keep the earth tidy and neat. The Neat is a well-developed app where you can share your food with people near you. Hereby, we want to accomplish less waste of food. We believe our small step will contribute a lot in this huge issue of food waste. Don’t waste your food and time, join us!

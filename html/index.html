Solution

Our solution based on the app; "NEAT" will give you the opportunity to reduce food waste in today's society, and that with only a few clicks. Do it at home, with a friend, or just drop by our food truck.

The App


The Neat, the app that will make sharing food easier, as well as reduce food waste. Use the app to share food with those near you, instead of throwing it away. On the app you can find lists of food near you, and you can even upload your own dishes, or any other groceries. You can even find our foodtrucks on the app where you can pick up more food.

About


We are The Neat, and our job is to do our best to keep the earth tidy and neat. The Neat is a well-developed app where you can share your food with people near you. Hereby, we want to accomplish less waste of food. We believe our small step will contribute a lot in this huge issue of food waste. Don’t waste your food and time, join us!

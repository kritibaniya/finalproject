Problem

Identifying a problem is not as easy as we think it is. A lot of time people are even finding solutions to problems that does not even exist. It is not the case this time. Food waste is a real and existing issue which is getting out of control every single day. We humans have created a mess that has a lot of negative impact and bad consequences. Crew of The Neat have done some research about why we should cut down wasting of valuable food. Click on read more to gather some knowledge on the importance of not throwing your food.

Solution

Do you have extra food left at home, and the food waste is their next destination? Share it! Give it to your neighbour, your friends, to your fellow citizens, and that, with The Neat! Help us reduce food waste together, with cooperation and friendship.

The App


The Neat, the app that will make sharing food easier, as well as reduce food waste. Use the app to share food with those near you, instead of throwing it away. On the app you can find lists of food near you, and you can even upload your own dishes, or any other groceries. You can even find our foodtrucks on the app where you can pick up more food.
